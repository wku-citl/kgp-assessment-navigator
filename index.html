As Kentucky's postsecondary institutions work to integrate the 10 Essential Skills into every academic program, one of the most important questions faculty face is:

How do I meaningfully assess these skills in my course?

This tool walks you through a short reflective process to help you identify assessment approaches that align with the essential skill you're addressing, the level at which your course addresses it, and what your learning outcomes ask students to do. The goal is not to prescribe a single right answer, but to offer practical options you can adapt for your discipline and your students.

Step 1: Select an Essential Skill

Choose the Essential Skill you'd like to explore assessment options for. If your course addresses multiple skills, you can return to explore additional skills after completing the process.
